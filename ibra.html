About Us

Our History

Founded in 2009, Ibra Foundation School Mirnga has been a beacon of educational excellence in the community. Starting with just 50 students and 5 teachers, we have grown into a comprehensive institution serving over 500 students from Nursery to Senior Secondary School.

Our name honors the legacy of educational philanthropy, and our motto "Knowledge is Light" guides everything we do. We are committed to providing quality, affordable education that prepares students for global citizenship.
