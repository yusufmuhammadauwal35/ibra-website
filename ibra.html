About Ibra Foundation School

Building Future Leaders Since 2009

Our History

Ibra Foundation School Mirnga was established in 2009 with a vision to provide quality education to the children of Mirnga and surrounding communities. What started as a small nursery school has grown into a comprehensive educational institution offering classes from Nursery 1 through SS 3.

Over the years, we have maintained our commitment to academic excellence, moral values, and holistic development of every child entrusted to our care.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures future leaders through quality education, moral values, and innovative learning.

Our Mission

To provide a conducive learning environment that promotes academic excellence, character development, and prepares students for higher education and life challenges.
